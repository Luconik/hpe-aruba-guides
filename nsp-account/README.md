<a name="fr"></a>
## 🇫🇷 Français

### Objectif

Ce guide explique comment créer un compte gratuit sur le **HPE Networking Support Portal (NSP)**.  
Ce compte est le prérequis pour accéder à la plateforme **HPE GreenLake** et au service **Aruba Central**.

> 📎 Ce même compte NSP permet aussi de télécharger les fichiers OVA (ex. AOS-CX Switch Simulator) pour EVE-NG — voir [`homelab-setup/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/`](https://github.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/tree/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/).

---

### Prérequis

- Une adresse e-mail **professionnelle ou sur domaine personnalisé** (ex. `@votre-domaine.com`)  
  ⚠️ Les adresses Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, etc. ne sont **pas acceptées**
- Un navigateur web moderne

---

### Étape 1 — Accéder au portail et créer un compte

1. Aller sur [https://networkingsupport.hpe.com](https://networkingsupport.hpe.com)
2. Cliquer sur **"Créer un compte"**

![NSP - Page d'accueil](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_account_creation.png)

---

### Étape 2 — Démarrer l'onboarding (6 étapes)

| # | Étape | Description |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 1 | **Start** | Introduction et présentation des étapes |
| 2 | **Email Validation** | Vérification de l'adresse e-mail via lien d'activation |
| 3 | **Select Primary Account** | Sélection ou création d'un compte organisation |
| 4 | **Company Validation** | Validation des informations de l'entreprise |
| 5 | **User Information** | Adresse personnelle + acceptation EULA |
| 6 | **End** | Confirmation d'onboarding terminé |

Cliquer sur **"Start Onboarding"** pour commencer.

![NSP - Start Onboarding](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Start_onboarding.png)

---

### Étape 3 — Validation de l'e-mail

Un e-mail de vérification est envoyé à l'adresse renseignée. Le lien est valable **6 heures**.

![NSP - Email Validation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Email_validation.png)

Cliquer sur le lien reçu par e-mail (objet : *HPE Networking Support Portal Email Verification*).

![NSP - Mail de confirmation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Mail_Confirmation.png)

Une fois le lien cliqué : **"Verified! Your email is now validated."** → Cliquer **Continue**.

![NSP - Email Verified](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Email_verified.png)

---

### Étape 4 — Sélectionner ou créer un compte organisation

Si aucun compte ne correspond à votre domaine, cliquer **"Create"**.

![NSP - Select Primary Account](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Select_Primary_Account.png)

Renseigner un **Display Name** et une description, puis cliquer **Create**.

![NSP - Create Account](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Create_Account.png)

---

### Étape 5 — Validation de l'entreprise

Vérifier et compléter les champs (Company Name, Country, City, Postal Code, Address), puis cliquer **Create**.

![NSP - Company Validation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Company_Validation.png)

---

### Étape 6 — Informations utilisateur & EULA

Renseigner l'adresse postale, le nom d'utilisateur affiché, accepter le **End-User License Agreement**, puis cliquer **Next**.

![NSP - User Information](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_User_Information.png)

---

### Étape 7 — Onboarding terminé ✅

La page **"On-board Completed!"** confirme la création du compte.

![NSP - Onboarding Completed](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_End.png)

---

### Étape suivante

👉 Créer un workspace GreenLake et déployer Aruba Central : [`../greenlake-workspace/`](../greenlake-workspace/)

<a name="en"></a>
## 🇬🇧 English

### Purpose

This guide explains how to create a free account on the **HPE Networking Support Portal (NSP)**.  
This account is required to access the **HPE GreenLake** platform and the **Aruba Central** service.

> 📎 The same NSP account is also used to download OVA files (e.g. AOS-CX Switch Simulator) for EVE-NG — see [`homelab-setup/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/`](https://github.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/tree/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/).

---

### Prerequisites

- A **professional or custom-domain** email address (e.g. `@your-domain.com`)  
  ⚠️ Public providers (Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, etc.) are **not accepted**
- A modern web browser

---

### Step 1 — Access the portal and create an account

1. Go to [https://networkingsupport.hpe.com](https://networkingsupport.hpe.com)
2. Click **"Create an account"**

![NSP - Home page](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_account_creation.png)

---

### Step 2 — Start onboarding (6 steps)

| # | Step | Description |
|---|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Start** | Introduction and overview |
| 2 | **Email Validation** | Email verification via activation link |
| 3 | **Select Primary Account** | Select or create an organization account |
| 4 | **Company Validation** | Validate company information |
| 5 | **User Information** | Personal address + EULA acceptance |
| 6 | **End** | Onboarding confirmation |

Click **"Start Onboarding"** to begin.

![NSP - Start Onboarding](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Start_onboarding.png)

---

### Step 3 — Email validation

A verification email is sent. The link is valid for **6 hours**.

![NSP - Email Validation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Email_validation.png)

Click the link in the email (subject: *HPE Networking Support Portal Email Verification*).

![NSP - Confirmation email](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Mail_Confirmation.png)

Once clicked: **"Verified! Your email is now validated."** → Click **Continue**.

![NSP - Email Verified](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Email_verified.png)

---

### Step 4 — Select or create an organization account

If no account matches your domain, click **"Create"**.

![NSP - Select Primary Account](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Select_Primary_Account.png)

Enter a **Display Name** and description, then click **Create**.

![NSP - Create Account](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Create_Account.png)

---

### Step 5 — Company validation

Review and complete the fields, then click **Create**.

![NSP - Company Validation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_Company_Validation.png)

---

### Step 6 — User information & EULA

Enter your address, display username, accept the **End-User License Agreement**, then click **Next**.

![NSP - User Information](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_User_Information.png)

---

### Step 7 — Onboarding complete ✅

![NSP - Onboarding Completed](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Luconik/homelab-setup/main/eve-ng/aos-cx-ova/screenshots/NSP_End.png)

---
